OVM chargeable status

  • Cat A: Ordinarily a resident in the UK (according to the domestic Charging Regulations)
  • Cat B: Resident in the UK, in possession of a valid visa AND (surcharge paid OR exempt)
  • Cat C: Ordinarily resident in another EEA country AND (EHIC/PRC/S2 OR exempt)
  • Cat D: Ordinarily resident in another EEA country AND (No EHIC/PRC/S2 OR not exempt)
  • Cat E: Not ordinarily resident in the UK, not paid, but exempt by personal status or treatment
  • Cat F: Not ordinarily resident in the UK or EEA, not paid, not exempt by personal status or treatment
